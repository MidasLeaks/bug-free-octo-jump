ARMS RACE


Arms Race is an online multiplayer first-person shooter with competitive team based game play, created in Epic Games Unreal Engine 4.

PLAY UP TO 5 DIFFERENT GAME MODES.

Arms Race offers competitive game play with a huge focus on team play. With up to 32 players online, you can dive in solo or with friends and have the same amazing experience, with game modes like "Invasion" which puts two factions against each other fighting to take control of points on the map. Players will have a range of weapons, vehicles and explosive's to their disposal to ensure victory.
